Something in the Well

(2024 Feb - 2024 May)

A super short film Directed and Animated by Evelyn Cheung .

This project explores short form storytelling, mixed media and paper cut-out animation.

All the backgrounds were traditionally inked and hand painted using Photoshop.

Story:

"Two travelers flee a mountain monster and hide in a well. Tempted by greed, one is drawn to a coin pond and meets a tragic end."
