Paper Reflections

(2025 Feb - 2025 May)

a Documentary Film exploring mother and child relationships and expressing memories through paper cut-out animation.

Took on main roles such as the Producer, Concept Artist, Animator and more.

Story:

"Two grown-up children's reflections on memories with their mothers; exploring the nuances of the mother and child experience through documentary"

Process (more below):

All the backgrounds were cut-out and hand painted (watercolour), majority of the rough animation were traditionally animated, cut-out, coloured (pencil colour) and shot in the Stop-motion studio.
